08 / FAQs

How is wood pressed oil different from refined oil? +

Wood pressed oils are extracted without heat or chemicals, preserving their natural nutrients and flavor, whereas refined oils undergo heavy processing.

Is JIRASO oil chemical-free? +

Yes, all JIRASO oils are 100% natural, unrefined, and free from any chemical processing or preservatives.

Can I use it for daily cooking? +

Absolutely. They are perfect for daily meals due to their high nutritional value and authentic taste.

Does it retain nutrients? +

Yes, the traditional wood-press method generates minimal heat, keeping vital nutrients, antioxidants, and natural fatty acids intact.

Which oil is best for health? +

It depends on your cooking style. Mustard is great for traditional dishes, Sesame for wellness, and Groundnut as a healthy, versatile option for everyday cooking.
