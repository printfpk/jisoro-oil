What is Lakdi Ghani?

Understanding the traditional wood pressed method passed down through generations.

100%
Pure

Traditional Roots

Lakdi Ghani is the ancient Indian method of extracting oil using a wooden press (ghani). This slow, natural process honors traditional wisdom.

True Cold Pressed

The temperature never exceeds 50°C during extraction. This gentle processing ensures all vital nutrients, vitamins, and minerals remain completely intact.

Zero Chemicals

Absolutely no chemicals, hexanes, or solvents are used in our extraction. What you get is 100% pure, unadulterated natural goodness.

The Difference

Wood Pressed vs Refined

Wood Pressed

  • Nutrients IntactVitamins, minerals, and antioxidants are fully preserved.
  • Natural AromaRetains the authentic flavor and smell of the original seeds.
  • Chemical FreeNo solvents or chemical bleaching applied at any stage.
  • Heart HealthyExcellent Omega-3 to Omega-6 balance for cardiovascular health.

Refined Oil

  • Nutrients DestroyedHigh heat processing (200°C+) eliminates most health benefits.
  • Chemical ExtractionUses Hexane and other chemical solvents to maximize yield.
  • Bland & TastelessDeodorization processes strip away all natural properties.
  • Trans FatsHydrogenation often introduces unhealthy trans fats.

Backed by Science

Clinical studies consistently prove that cold-pressed extraction preserves vital micronutrients, offering significantly higher antioxidant levels compared to conventionally refined counterparts.
