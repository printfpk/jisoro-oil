FAQs

Common Questions About Our Oils

Everything you need to know about wood-pressed oils and why they outperform refined alternatives in taste, nutrition, and purity.

How is wood pressed oil different from refined? +

Wood pressed oils are extracted without heat or chemicals, preserving their natural nutrients and flavour. Refined oils undergo intensive industrial processing that destroys most nutritional value.

Is JIRASO oil completely chemical-free? +

Yes - 100%. All JIRASO oils are unrefined and free from hexane, preservatives, or any synthetic additives. What you get is pure oil, exactly as nature intended.

Can I use it for daily cooking? +

Absolutely - they're designed for everyday use. High nutritional value and authentic taste make them perfect for frying, tempering, sautÃ©ing, and even raw dressings.

Does it retain its nutrients? +

Yes. The traditional wood-press method generates minimal heat keeping vital nutrients, antioxidants and natural fatty acids intact - unlike high-heat industrial processing.

Which oil is best for my health? +

Mustard for bold traditional cooking, Sesame for wellness & South Indian cuisine, Coconut for tropical cooking & skincare, Groundnut for a light daily all-rounder.
